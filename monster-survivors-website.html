About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game inspired by popular roguelite titles. Players must survive against endless waves of monsters while collecting experience and upgrades to become increasingly powerful.

The game features a variety of characters, each with their own unique starting weapons and abilities. As you progress, you'll unlock new weapons, passive items, and power-ups that can be combined in countless ways to create powerful builds.

With its simple controls but deep strategy, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. Each run is different, with procedurally generated environments and enemy patterns keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

Whether you have a few minutes or want to dive in for hours, Monster Survivors offers thrilling gameplay that will keep you coming back for "just one more run!"
